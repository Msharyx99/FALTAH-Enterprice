# Upgrade Guide — Release 4.2

1. Backup/commit your current working version.
2. Extract this ZIP.
3. Copy all files into `FALTAH-Enterprise`.
4. Replace old files.
5. Commit: `Release 4.2 local CMS restored`.
6. Push origin.
7. Open your website and go to Admin CMS.

Do not delete your `FALTAH-Knowledge` or `FALTAH-Media` repositories.
